


\begin{abstract}
Abstract is a short overview of the content of the work. The main goal of abstract is to give a reader an idea about the work, without the need to read it all. Thus, the abstract should be concise, but at the same time concrete on the content of the work.

Typically, abstract is not more than one page long, and presents the work in a brief and concise way. Often abstract follows the outline of the work presents a problem, method, and results.
\end{abstract}
